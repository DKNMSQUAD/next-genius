Dear Counsellor,

Every year, brilliant, hard-working students from middle-income families never get a real shot at a leading global college, simply because no one opened the door. Whether you guide them in your school or in your own practice, you see these students every day. We would like you to nominate one.

The Next Genius Scholarship awards full and partial tuition scholarships to select high-performing, middle-income high school students from India to attend leading global colleges. Nominees go through a guided assessment, college discovery, and interview journey, fully supported, at no cost to the student or the school.

If a deserving student comes to mind, take a moment to nominate them. It takes two minutes, and it could change the entire course of their life.
