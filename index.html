Climate change. Rise of Robotic Automation & Artificial Intelligence. Unclear Digital Privacy. Resource Limitations. Deglobalization. Job shortages. Human society seems to be at a crossroads. Clearly, novel problems need novel solutions. A global education makes more sense today than ever before.

At Next Genius, we award full and partial tuition scholarships to select high-performing, middle-income high school students from South Asia to attend leading global colleges. Today, we foster a community of 351 scholar changemakers.
