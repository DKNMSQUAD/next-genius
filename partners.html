The students our partners keep asking for.

Our partner colleges tell us the same thing, year after year. Next Genius scholars arrive with a strong work ethic, they behave well, and they become leaders and volunteers on campus. They do not sit quietly at the edge of college life.

Many go on to work inside the admissions office itself, sitting in on information sessions and giving campus tours to the next set of families walking through the gate.

Some of the most promising students in our community, and among the most active. Several of them now represent the college to prospective families. Feedback from partner college admissions teams

Why we are waiving the fee

The international admissions community has backed the growth of this program in a very big way. At a time when the international environment is difficult for students and for colleges alike, this is our way of giving something back.
