College Allocation

Reviewed and assigned by the Next Genius team.

Your college choices have been submitted. The Next Genius team is reviewing your profile and matching you with the most suitable partner college based on your preferences, scores, profile fit, and college capacity.

You'll be notified here and via email once an allocation is made. Typical turnaround: 5–10 working days.

Group Interview

Joint interview with the Next Genius team and your allocated college's admissions officer.

Once your individual interview videos are reviewed, you'll be invited to a group interview slot. The Next Genius team and a representative from your allocated college will join.

What to expect

  • ~60–90 minutes online (Zoom/Meet link will be shared)
  • 5 minute introduction from each panelist
  • 15 minute Q&A on your profile, motivation and fit
  • Time for you to ask questions about the college
  • Casual close — no formal scoring during the call

Final Decision

The outcome of your scholarship application.

After the group interview, the Next Genius team and the partner college finalise their decision. You'll see the result here as soon as it's available.

Exit the Scholarship Process

This action permanently deletes your account and all data.

If you choose to exit, we will:

  • Permanently delete your registration, profile, test scores, photo, and all uploaded files
  • Remove you from any college allocation pools
  • Stop sending all emails, WhatsApp messages, and calls
  • This is irreversible — you cannot rejoin with the same email
